Welcome to We Five

We Five Orchestra Group is for anyone or group who loves and respect music, especially for one who get inspired by music and loves music the most when it is played in a group. This orchestral group was created by a group of five for the aim of making orchestral music and available to people, and bringing out the best out of people who play orchestral music.

In this page anyone or group can upload his music as part of the aim of making orchestral music available for people, also provisions for videos of orcrhestral musics and tuorials on how to play orchestral musical are made available, questions, answers and comments are provided to clear peoples difficulties and confusion in orchestral music and its instrument and to make it enjoyable.

Our History

The current We Five is made up of a group of five (three men and two women) who met on a talent show for orchestral music. Originally the members of we five were members of the top five diffrent groups of the show that emerged as the best. The five united after the organizers of the show picked the best five from the groups for the aim of bringing out the best out of them and making orchestral music available and enjoyable for people.

The members of We Five are successed every year in all its branch around the world.Just as those who selected us were from a group of five and we were also selected from a group of five we will also select from a group of five and the succession continues every year.

Our Services

We organise orchestral talent show to select the best five to win the We Five prize that starts from having a record album of selected best five with top orchestral music stars and other sponsorships, and the progression continues every year for We Five Orchestral Group. We also make provisions of videos of orcrhestral musics and tuorials on how to play orchestral musical instruments, and we organise questions and answers on orchestral music to make sure that the culture of orchestral music is not replaces with a new trend of music.
